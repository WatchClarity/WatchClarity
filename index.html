Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you guarantee authenticity?
No — we assess based on listing photos and descriptions. For full peace of mind on a high-value purchase, always follow up with an in-person check by a certified watchmaker.
How fast will I get my report?
Within 24 hours of receiving your listing and payment. Faster turnaround is available on request — just ask when you submit.
Which platforms do you cover?
Any online listing — eBay, Chrono24, Watchfinder, Subdial, private dealers, physical stores. If there's a URL or photos, we can work with it.
Is this AI-generated?
No. Every report is written by Midas — a real collector with 10+ years in the pre-owned market. No automation, no templates.
How much does it cost?
£25 per listing report. £25 per sourcing request. That's it — no hidden fees, no subscription required.
